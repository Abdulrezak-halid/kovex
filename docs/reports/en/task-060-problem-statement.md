# TASK-060 - Academic Problem Statement

## Purpose

This document defines the business problem addressed by Kovex ERP in clear academic language. The English version is intended for GitHub issue summaries, repository documentation, and project summaries.

## Problem Statement

Small and medium-sized businesses often manage sales, purchasing, inventory, customers, suppliers, and reporting through separate tools. These tools may include spreadsheets, paper records, messaging applications, and basic accounting software. Because the information is distributed across different places, businesses struggle to maintain a single reliable source of operational data.

This fragmented workflow creates problems in stock tracking, order management, invoice follow-up, and business reporting. The same information may be entered more than once, records may become outdated, and important operational details may be missed. As a result, businesses can experience wasted time, human error, delayed decisions, and reduced operational efficiency.

## Importance

For SMEs, organized and traceable business processes are important for growth and sustainability. Incorrect stock information may lead to overstocking or product shortages. Poorly tracked sales and purchasing data makes revenue, expense, and performance analysis more difficult. A centralized and easy-to-use ERP approach can help SMEs manage daily operations with better control and visibility.

## Beneficiaries

The primary beneficiaries are SME owners, managers, and operational employees. Sales, purchasing, inventory, accounting, and planning staff can work with shared data from the same system. Customers and suppliers may also benefit indirectly through more consistent order, invoice, and stock processes.

## Current Alternatives

Common alternatives include spreadsheets, manual tracking, general business tools, and large ERP platforms. Spreadsheets are flexible but limited in data integrity, permission control, and process automation. Large ERP systems can be expensive, complex, or excessive for SME needs. Kovex ERP aims to provide a simpler, modular, and understandable business management experience focused on SME operations.
